Delete Your Nexa Account

If you would like to delete your Nexa account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@nexaapp.com from your registered email address
  2. Use the subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered phone number

We will process your request within 72 hours.

Data Deletion Details:

  • Your personal information will be deleted
  • Your transaction history may be retained where required for legal or regulatory purposes
